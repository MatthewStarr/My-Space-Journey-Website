My Space Adventure

Can't Come In Today



I don’t feel so good. It would appear that as I did no training whatsoever before coming on this trip my body had no idea what to expect. It’s like when you go on holiday to a tropical new land and you’re not immune to the diseases it has, except it’s in space. Dr. Bomki says I have a type of flu called Moonbonic plague, but it’s not as serious as it sounds. I just have to take some painkillers and apply sudocrem twice daily. I have quarantined though to make sure no one else on board gets ill. If I get any worse they’re under strict instructions to shoot me into space so I don’t compromise the mission. I’m hoping it won’t come to that.
