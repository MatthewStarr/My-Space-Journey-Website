Back To Earth



It’s finally time to return home. We’ve had some good times but the time has come to land back on Earth. We’ve just passed the Moon so we should be back in a couple of days. As our mission comes to a close we’ve all been reflecting on the last couple of months. It has been a once in a lifetime experience for all of us and we’re sad to join the Earth’s atmosphere again. You get used to zero gravity. I don’t want to not be able to float like a chump. Maybe i’ll be invited to go on another mission as I didn’t screw up this one.

I wonder where we’ll land.
