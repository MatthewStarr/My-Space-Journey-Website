Fixing The Ship



If you read my last post you know we had a slight mishap involving an asteroid. The engineers have been working hard over the last couple of days and the ship is repaired. This wasn’t some crappy rush job either, it’s properly fixed. They grabbed their tool boxes and faced the black abyss of space. The asteroid hit one of the wings of the ship so we’ve just been going in circles because we couldn’t go in a straight line, but now it’s fixed we can now continue our exploration of the galaxy.
